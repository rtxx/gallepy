Usage

Using gallepy is simple:
  • Click on a thumbnail.
  • Click the arrows to navigate.
  • Click on the center of the image do download it.

About

gallepy is a simple gallery made with python and htmx. I made it in an atempt to learn flask and htmx. To use it, simply put your photos on the gallery folder and that's it!

It has some features, like:

  • Thumbnails are created for each image automatically
  • Lazy loading
  • Infinite scroll
  • Smooth CSS animations
